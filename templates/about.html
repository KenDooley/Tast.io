Tast.io

Contact

Tast.io ("Tasty-oh") is a restaurant discovery tool that uses natural language processing techniques on restaurant review text from Yelp in order to derive its suggestions.

This was my final project from Zipfian Academy and began as a way to address the following problem: When in a new city, how do you find restaurants most similar to a favorite restaurant in your hometown?

While it's certainly possible to search restaurant review sites for places serving a certain cuisine at a certain price point with a certain rating, it's more difficult to capture some of the more subtle aspects of what makes an establishment special.

Some restaurants have amazing waterfront views, while others offer views of a skyline.   Some pizza is Chicago style, some pizza is New York style, and sometimes pizza connoisseurs want something more artisanal.   Cozy?  Romantic?   Loud?   Fun?  Tast.io is able to tease out these qualities from what people are saying on Yelp.  If you would like to find out more about how it works, please visit my github link in the contact page.

How to Use:

1)   In the search bar, input the name of a restaurant located in New York City or San Francisco for which you would like to find the closest matches.

2)   Select the restaurant from the autocomplete list.

3)   From the first drop-down list, select the city in which you would like to find similar restaurants

4)   If you live in San Francisco or New York and you have chosen to receive results in your city, you can also choose to receive results within a certain distance of you. (Must allow location services)
